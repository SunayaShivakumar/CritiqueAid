What is CritiqueAid?

CritiqueAid is a Google Chrome extension for the design_critiques subreddit that aids in writing meaningful, constructive critiques through exposure to real, example critiques.

Set your preferences below:

Select design areas that you care about.

CritiqueAid will show you example critiques related to selected design areas.

CritiqueAid

About

CritiqueAid is Google Chrome extension for the design_critiques subreddit that aids in writing meaningful, constructive feedback and critique by providing good and bad examples to the user.

How to use CritiqueAid

Simply search for keywords like "resume", or "portfolio", depending on the subject matter that you are critiquing, and you will be shown a good sample critique. If you click on the "bad" option in the bottom toolbar, you will be shown a bad sample critique. Happy critiquing!
